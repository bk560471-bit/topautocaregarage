Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, booking process, payments, and more. If you don't see what you're looking for, get in touch – we're here to help!

General

What services does Top Autocare Garage offer?

We provide comprehensive automotive services including routine maintenance (oil changes, tire rotations), diagnostics, engine repairs, brake services, electrical work, and more. Visit our Services page for full details and pricing.

Where is your garage located?

We're conveniently located at Milestone Business Centre, Total Energies Ridgeways, Kiambu Rd, Nairobi. See our Contact page for a map and directions.

What are your operating hours?

Mon-Fri: 8AM-6PM, Sat: 8AM-4PM. We're closed on Sundays and public holidays. Appointments outside hours may be arranged – contact us.

Booking & Appointments

How do I book an appointment?

Sign up or log in, then navigate to the Book Appointment page. Select your service, vehicle, date, and time. You'll receive instant confirmation via email or SMS.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes, up to 24 hours before your appointment without charge. Use the Appointments page or contact us. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

What if I'm running late?

We'll accommodate if possible, but your service time may be shortened. Aim to arrive 15 minutes early. Call +254 757 562 447 if delayed.

Do I need to pre-register my vehicle?

It's helpful but not required. Add vehicles via My Vehicles for quicker bookings. On arrival, we'll assist with details.

Services & Pricing

How accurate are price estimates?

Estimates are based on standard work. Actual costs may vary after diagnosis. We'll get your approval for any extras. All prices include VAT.

What payment options are available?

Cash, M-Pesa, Visa/Mastercard. Pay after service. Deposits for major jobs. No processing fees.

Do you use genuine parts?

Yes, OEM or equivalent from verified suppliers. We discuss options and offer 3-12 month warranties.

What about major repairs?

Detailed quotes provided. Deposits for jobs over KSh 50,000. Towing available extra.

Payments & Refunds

Is there a booking fee?

No upfront fees. Pay upon completion. Deposits for large jobs only.

What's your refund policy?

Full refunds up to 24 hours before; 50% 12-24 hours. Completed services non-refundable but warrantied. Contact us for issues.

Account & Privacy

How secure is my information?

Data encrypted via Firebase, compliant with Kenya's Data Protection Act. No sharing/selling. View Privacy Policy.

Forgot password?

Use "Forgot Password" on sign-in, or email support.

Can I delete my account?

Request via profile. Personal data removed; records retained 5 years for compliance.
