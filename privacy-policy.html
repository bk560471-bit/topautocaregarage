Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Top Autocare Garage ("we," "us," or "our"). We operate the website topautocare.com (the "Site") and provide automotive services in Nairobi, Kenya.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our Site, use our services (including online booking), or contact us. By using the Site or our services, you consent to the practices described here.

We comply with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 and other applicable privacy laws. If you have questions, contact us at bk560471@gmail.com or +254 757 562 447.

Note: This policy applies to all users, including those booking appointments and managing vehicles online.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information you provide when creating an account, booking services, or contacting us:

  • Account Details: Name, email address, phone number, and password (hashed).
  • Vehicle Information: Make, model, year, VIN (Vehicle Identification Number), color, and license plate.
  • Appointment Data: Service type, preferred date/time, notes, and vehicle selection.
  • Contact Form Submissions: Name, email, subject, and message from the contact page.
  • Payment Information: If integrated (future), we use third-party processors (e.g., M-Pesa) and do not store card details.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit the Site:

  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device info, pages visited, and time spent (via Firebase Analytics).
  • Cookies: Small files for functionality (e.g., session management) and analytics (Google Analytics). We do not use tracking cookies for advertising.
  • Location Data: Approximate location from IP for service area relevance (not precise GPS).

We do not collect sensitive data (e.g., health, financial) beyond what's necessary for automotive services.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process and manage appointments, vehicle registrations, and service requests.
  • Send confirmations, reminders, and updates via email/SMS (e.g., appointment status changes).
  • Improve our services through analytics (e.g., popular services, site usage).
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Customize user experience (e.g., vehicle recommendations based on your fleet).
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g., records for Kenyan vehicle regulations).

We store data on secure Firebase servers (Google Cloud) with encryption at rest and in transit.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only when necessary:

  • Service Providers: Firebase/Google for hosting/auth/analytics; SMS providers for notifications (e.g., appointment reminders).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or Kenyan authorities (e.g., Traffic Department for vehicle data).
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger/acquisition, with notification.
  • Your Consent: For marketing (you can opt-out anytime).

No sharing with third-party marketers or advertisers without explicit consent.

Data Security

We prioritize your data security:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted via HTTPS; stored encrypted in Firebase.
  • Access Controls: Authenticated users only access their data (admin views anonymized reports).
  • Firebase Security: Industry-standard protections; regular audits.
  • Retention: Personal data kept as long as your account is active; appointment history for 5 years (legal compliance); deleted upon request.

While we use reasonable measures, no online transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Data Protection Rights

Under Kenyan Data Protection Act and GDPR principles, you have rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data (email us).
  • Correction: Update inaccurate info via profile/vehicles pages.
  • Deletion: Request account/vehicle deletion (permanently removes data).
  • Objection: Opt-out of marketing/emails (unsubscribe links).
  • Portability: Export your data in structured format.

To exercise rights, email bk560471@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days.

For Data Protection Officer complaints: Contact Kenya's Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC).

Cookies and Tracking

We use:

  • Essential Cookies: For Site functionality (e.g., login sessions) – cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics (via Firebase Measurement ID) for usage stats – anonymized IP.
  • No Advertising Cookies: We do not track for ads.

Manage cookies via browser settings. Disabling may affect Site features. Learn more at Google's Cookie Policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in practices or law. Significant updates will be emailed to users and posted on the Site with a new "Last Updated" date.

Continued use after changes means acceptance. Check periodically.
